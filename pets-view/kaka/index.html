Meet Ka-ka

Meet the easiest cat to fall in love with, Ka-Ka!

This sweet and friendly feline is looking for his forever home!! Ka-Ka is a five month old seven pound male that is neutered and vaccinated. Currently, he is still on eye medication due to an eye infection he received when he was younger. In devotion to Ka-Ka, further information on cat eye infections are provided on the next slide.

Ka-Ka was rescued at four weeks old, when his mother was killed in a car accident. After his experience of being independent, starting from a young age, we are looking for your care and commitment to help him in his forever at home journey.
