Meet Bao

Meet Bao the Samoyed!!

Bao is a three year old snow white female Samoyed and weighs around 50 pounds. She is spayed and is up to date with vaccinations. She is fully housebroken as she had previously lived in a more urban setting, a small apartment. Due to her size and the need for her to have a larger space, her previous owner had released her for new adoption. Bao is a super gentle, playful and loving creature (just look at that face and tongue). She absolutely loves to play with humans, including kids. In addition, Bao gets along with other pets very well. She loves taking walks in the backyard and around the neighborhood. She is generally fed twice a day and takes her treats very gently.

Bao is currently with a foster family in central NJ. We can travel within a two hour drive from central NJ to meet new potential adopters. We are looking for a loving family for Bao and are very excited for the new chapter of her story. The adoption fee is $650.
