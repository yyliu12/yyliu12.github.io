Meet Lele the Sweet!

This wonderful specimen of sweetness is looking to steal your heart! Lele is a 3-year-old female mixed breed dog. She is loving and sweet, yet calm and independent. She will gently ask for your attention as she grabs the hearts of other pets by getting along so well with them.

Lele is not a high energy dog. Weighing at about 10 pounds, she might not be able to run laps with you with her little legs. But there is no room for underestimation since she makes the perfect companion for a family that wants a friendly, gentle, and calm pet.
